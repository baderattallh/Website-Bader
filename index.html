Water Section

It is difficult to obtain water in Gaza because it is cut off by the Israeli occupation. Even if it is available, citizens must stand in line to obtain a gallon of water. Because of this, diseases are spreading in Gaza.

Potable Water

To get potable water you have to go to a faraway area to get one gallon for $2.

Electricity Section

Electricity is very difficult to obtain, and if you do get it, it's expensive. A kilowatt of electricity sells for $8.50, which is expensive for Gazans. There is another way: alternative energy, the most popular of which is solar energy. Batteries are very expensive, and a complete system requires $40,000 due to the trade.

Food Section

As for food, it is another story because since the beginning of the Nazi war, food shortages have been present and severe. Children are dying of hunger and disease, and they need food. Where is the world?

These words come from the heart of the suffering of the Gazan people.
